## The Assignment

Watch and analyze Brian Little's TED Talk "Who are you, really? The puzzle of personality"

The purpose of this exercise is to improve your ability to listen actively
and analyze a speech — focusing on structure, transitions, sources, and delivery.

**As you watch:**
- Listen for the overall idea — what is he trying to convey about personality?
- Notice the three-point structure (introduction, body, conclusion) and how each point flows.
- Pay attention to transitions & signposting — those verbal cues that keep the audience on track.
- Observe references (does he cite studies or personal anecdotes?), and ask how credible or persuasive they are.
- Pay close attention to the delivery — body language, tone, rhetorical questions, how he connects with the audience.

Watch the whole TED-talk once to understand the main idea of the speech.

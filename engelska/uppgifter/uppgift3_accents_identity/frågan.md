## The Assignment

### Part B: Source Critical Evaluation

Write a source critical evaluation of the third source you used in your
informative speech. Use the CRAAP method to analyze the source.

**Length:** 300–400 words.

**Include the following:**

**Currency**
- When was the source published?
- Is this date appropriate for your topic? Explain why.

**Relevance**
- How did this source help your speech?
- Which specific parts or quotes did you use?
- Why were these parts useful for your topic?
- Give exact examples from the source that supported your points in the speech.

**Authority**
- Who wrote the source?
- What are their qualifications?
- Where was it published?
- How do you know the publisher is reliable?
- Give specific details about why you trust this source.

**Accuracy**
- What evidence does the source use?
- Did you check these facts in other sources?
- What kind of research backs up the claims?
- Give examples of: Data used, research cited, expert opinions quoted.
- Explain how you verified this information.

**Purpose**
- Why was this source created?
- Who is the intended audience?
- Is there any bias?
- Support your answers with details from the source.
